About Samukchara High School, Tripura

Samukchara High School is run by the Department of Education, Government of Tripura. Established in 1995, it is a civil sector school catering to the educational needs of the students of Tripura. Initially started for the wards of Government Employees, it is now one of the largest schools in Tripura.

This is a two-section school with a double-storey building and two extension blocks for the Primary Classes. The classrooms are well ventilated, equipped with suitable furniture for different age groups, and provided with proper civic facilities.

The school operates under the control of I/S Kakraban and is guided by the School Management Committee (SMC) at the local level in accordance with SCERT rules for the smooth running of the school.
